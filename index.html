ABOUT THE CLUB

NAC is a creative club dedicated to exploring and sharing the art and culture of animation (Anime).

We are not only a gathering place for enthusiasts, but also a vibrant community. Here, through regular screenings, in-depth discussions and various themed activities, we connect all the souls who love the culture of anime. Whether you are a seasoned manga fan or just beginning to explore this imaginative world, NAC welcomes your participation.
